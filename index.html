Let's Chat About Your Move

Why I Do This

Hi, I'm Ngaire. I moved to Ningbo in 2022 with my husband and two young sons.

Here's what makes me a bit unusual: I actually love the things most people hate about moving. Apartment hunting? I find it exciting. Unpacking boxes? Therapeutic. Organising spaces? My idea of a good time.

I spent years managing complex software projects, which taught me how to break overwhelming tasks into manageable pieces. Turns out that skill translates perfectly to helping people move and organise their homes.

I have some other main priorities in life, but I genuinely love helping people with moving and organising projects. There's something incredibly satisfying about finding someone the right apartment, then helping them unpack and create a space where everything actually works.

If you're dreading your move or drowning in boxes - let me help. This is the stuff I enjoy.

How Can I Help?

How I Can Help

Finding Your Nest

Most people dread apartment hunting. I love it - the search, the negotiations, making sure you get the right place. I'll do the heavy lifting: searching online, vetting apartments, visiting places before you waste your time. Then I'll go with you to viewings, help with contracts, and get you moved in properly.

What's included:

  • Online search & agent communication
  • Pre-screening apartments (I only show you the good ones)
  • Accompanied viewings
  • Lease review and contract support
  • Police registration, fapiao setup, internet installation
  • Moving coordination or hands-on help

Investment: ¥500-1,200 per project

Creating Your Home

Unpacking boxes and creating an organized, functional home is my happy place. I use my project management background to tackle one room at a time - decluttering, organising, setting up systems that work for how you actually live. No judgment, just practical solutions.

What's included:

  • Full room organisation (kitchen, bedroom, kids' rooms, etc.)
  • Unpacking & setup for new arrivals
  • Storage solutions (using what you have or budget-friendly finds)
  • Cleaning & tidying as we organise
  • Systems that work for your lifestyle (and your ayi's!)
  • Follow-up to adjust anything that's not working

Investment: ¥100/hour (most rooms: ¥400-600)
